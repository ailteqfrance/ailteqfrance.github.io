Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. Information We Collect

AutoReel does not collect personal data from end users beyond what is strictly necessary to operate the service. When you authenticate via TikTok, we receive only the permissions you explicitly grant.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To publish videos to your TikTok account via the Content Posting API
  • To generate AI scripts and voiceovers based on selected topics
  • To improve the reliability and quality of the service

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except as required to operate the service (e.g., TikTok API, Pexels API for stock footage, Groq API for script generation).

4. Data Retention

We do not permanently store your TikTok credentials or personal information. Access tokens are used transiently during video publication and are not persisted.

5. Third-Party Services

AutoReel integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • TikTok for Developers – TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Pexels – stock video provider
  • Groq – AI script generation

6. Your Rights

You may revoke AutoReel's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's app settings under "Manage app permissions."
